About Me

Hi, my name is Anjaleena Barclay. I am a developer based in Orange County, California. I have always been interested in the way things work. In May of 2019, I graduated from Cal State University of Fullerton with a double major in business administration with a concentration in Information Systems and psychology.

In my information systems courses, I had the opportunity to learn how the foundation of information systems work. This included learning about the internet, databases and the software development life cycle. I had the opportunity to shadow an information technology department. During this time, I realized just how much learning I would get to do with a career in information systems. I decided that I would be most interested in learning about and being a part of the software development process.

I decided to attend an advanced full-stack web development program called LearningFuze. Through 12+ hour coding days at LearningFuze, I was able to take a closer look at the software development life cycle and learn new skills such as JavaScript, HTML, CSS. My experience at LearningFuze was amazing. Every day I felt excited to challenge myself, learn and create. I am excited to continue learning, growing and developing. I am open to any opportunities related to web and project development. Please feel free to connect with me.
